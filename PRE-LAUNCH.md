- Add the ABN to the `SITE` block near the top of `src/App.jsx` when ready.
- Test the Web3Forms contact form from the deployed Vercel URL.
- Confirm `ampd12v.com.au` is connected to the production Vercel project.
- Test the calculator on a phone and desktop.
- Walk through fridge, inverter and custom-appliance scenarios.
- Read the legal pages before launch; they are general templates, not legal advice.
- Submit `https://ampd12v.com.au/sitemap.xml` to Google Search Console after launch.
- Affiliate links and brand recommendations are intentionally excluded from this release.
